Tangit

Pipe Adhesive

High-strength solvent-based PVC-U pipe adhesive for permanent, leak-proof bonding of plastic piping systems.

Product Description

Tangit Pipe Adhesive 400g is a professional-grade cement designed for joining rigid PVC-U pipes and fittings. It works by chemically fusing pipe surfaces to create a strong, watertight, and pressure-resistant bond. It is commonly used in plumbing, drainage, irrigation, and conduit systems, ensuring durable joints that perform like the pipe material itself.

Key Features
  • Strong chemical (solvent) welding for PVC-U pipes
  • Provides leak-proof and pressure-resistant joints
  • Fast curing with short working time (about 4 minutes open time)
  • Suitable for water supply, drainage, and conduit systems
  • High durability and long-lasting bond strength
  • Resistant to pressure and normal water system temperatures
