OJO

Multi Silicone Sealant

100% neutral silicone designed to provide strong adhesion, flexibility, and long-lasting waterproof sealing. It is suitable for bathrooms, kitchens, windows, doors, and general construction joints. It resists mold, moisture, and weather changes, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor use.

Colors Available
Product Description
Key Features
  • 100% neutral cure silicone
  • Waterproof and highly flexible seal
  • Strong adhesion to glass, metal, aluminum, ceramic, wood, and concrete
  • Heat and weather resistant (-50°C to 150°C)
  • Suitable for indoor and outdoor applications
Direction for Use
  1. Clean and dry the surface completely.
  2. Cut nozzle at a 45° angle to desired bead size.
  3. Load into a sealant gun if applicable.
  4. Apply evenly along the joint or gap.
  5. Smooth the bead using a tool or soapy finger.
  6. Allow to cure fully before exposing to water or stress.
