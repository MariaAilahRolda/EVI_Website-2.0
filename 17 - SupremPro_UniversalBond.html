SupremPro

Universal Bond Nail Power

Download: SupremPro Catalog

SupremPro Universal Bond Nail Power is a high performance, solvent based adhesive, developed to bond a wide range of construction materials. Offering an instant tack and is suitable for indoor & outdoor use. It provides immediate bonding without requiring solvent flash-off. Works well in bonding brick, ceramic, concrete, hardboard, plywood, stone, MDF, vinyl and mirror.

Product Description

A high performance solvent based adhesive that delivers instant grab. No flashing required (solvent evaporation not needed). Suitable for both indoor and outdoor use.

  • Strong as nails
  • Instant grab & dry
  • Suits all weather

Ideal for bonding brick, ceramic, concrete, hardboard, MDF, plywood, vinyl, and mirror glass.

Directions for use
  • Surface must be dry, clean and free from dust and grease.
  • Cut off tip of nozzle at desired bead and angle.
  • Apply adhesive and press firmly for 10–20 seconds.
  • Heavy objects may require support.
Tips
  1. Use spots for uneven surfaces
  2. Use wave pattern for wide surfaces
  3. Use straight lines for small surfaces
  4. Not suitable for PE, PP, Teflon, Styrofoam
  5. Does not stain mirror backing
Setting Time

Approx. 24 hours

Coverage/Cartridge

Approx. 12m at 5mm beads
