Product Description

Ecozmart EcoBoards Plus are engineered composite panels made from recycled multi-layer packaging materials (typically aluminum + plastic layers from beverage cartons) that are processed into strong, rigid sheet boards. These boards are designed as an eco-friendly alternative to plywood, MDF, and other traditional wood-based panels.
They are commonly used in interior construction, cabinetry, wall cladding, partitions, and furniture fabrication due to their durability, moisture resistance, and sustainability.
EcoBoard Plus materials are known for being part of circular economy solutions, transforming waste packaging into usable building materials.

Key Features
  • Eco-Friendly / Recycled Material - Made from recycled laminated packaging waste.
  • Moisture & Water Resistant - Does not easily absorb water.
  • Strong & Durable - High structural strength compared to ordinary plastic sheets, Resistant to cracking, warping, and delamination
  • Termite & Rot Proof - Not affected by termites, fungi, or rot (unlike wood materials)
  • Versatile Applications - Cabinetry and wardrobes, Wall panels and partitions, Furniture boards & Ceiling backing panels
