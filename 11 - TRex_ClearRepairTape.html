T-Rex®

Ferociously Strong Clear Repair Tape

Crystal-clear heavy-duty repair tape for strong, waterproof, and discreet indoor or outdoor repairs.

Product Description

T-REX Clear Repair Tape is an all-weather repair tape designed with aggressive adhesive strength and a transparent finish for nearly invisible repairs. It bonds to rough and wet surfaces while providing a durable waterproof and UV-resistant seal, making it ideal for home, automotive, camping, and outdoor applications.

Product Description
  • Aggressive adhesive sticks to wet surfaces
  • All-weather for use on indoor and outdoor repairs
  • Applies at below freezing temperatures
  • Hand tear-able for easy application
  • Waterproof backing for water-resistant repairs (after application to surface)
