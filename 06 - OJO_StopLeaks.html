OJO

Stop Leaks Waterproof Spray

Ready-to-use acrylic waterproof spray that instantly seals leaks, cracks, and joints for long-lasting water protection.

Product Description

OJO Stop Leaks Waterproof Spray is a high-performance acrylic coating designed to stop water leakage and seal damaged surfaces. It adheres strongly to materials such as metal, concrete, wood, brick, PVC, and fiberglass, even on wet surfaces. It forms a flexible, durable, and UV-resistant waterproof layer suitable for roofs, gutters, walls, pipes, and other repair areas. Ideal for emergency and long-term waterproofing applications.

Key Features
  • Instant waterproof sealing for cracks and leaks
  • Instant waterproof sealing for cracks and leaks
  • Strong adhesion to multiple materials (metal, concrete, wood, PVC, etc.)
  • UV and weather resistant for outdoor durability
  • Anti-fungal protection for longer-lasting repair
Direction for Use
  1. Clean the surface and remove loose dirt, dust, or oil.
  2. Shake can well for about 1 minute before use.
  3. Spray from about 20 cm distance with a thin first coat.
  4. Apply 2–3 coats for best sealing performance, allowing time between layers.
  5. Let cure for around 24 hours before exposing to full water pressure.
