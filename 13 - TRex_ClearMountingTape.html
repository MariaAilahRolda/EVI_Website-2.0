T-Rex®

Strong and Clear Mounting Tape

Extra-thick clear double-sided mounting tape for strong, permanent bonding without nails or screws.

Product Description

T-REX Clear Mounting Tape Extra Thick is a heavy-duty transparent double-sided adhesive designed for strong and permanent mounting applications. It provides a discreet bonding solution that holds securely on a wide range of surfaces such as metal, glass, wood, tile, brick, and concrete. Ideal for indoor and outdoor use, it replaces nails, screws, and glue while maintaining a clean, invisible finish.

Based on product specifications, it features a thick acrylic adhesive layer designed for high-strength load holding and long-lasting durability, with weather and UV resistance for outdoor applications.

Key Features
  • Transparent, double sided tape is .039 inches thick and holds up to 30 lbs. per roll or 4 inches per each 2 lbs. of weight for discrete, heavy duty mounting applications.
  • Permanent application that works on a variety of surfaces including metal, glass, wood, concrete, brick, tile and ceramic for an easy alternative to nails, screws, staples and glue.
  • All weather mounting tape withstands high temperatures up to 200˚F (93˚C) for extreme hold during indoor/outdoor use and is weather & UV resistant for year-long durability.
  • BEST RESULTS: Ensure surfaces are clean, dry and free of moisture before applying. Apply firm pressure to double sided tape to enable the strong adhesive bond with surfaces. T-Rex Mounting Tape adhesive will build over time, achieving full strength after a 24 hour period.
  • Will not stick to all grades of plastic, including polyethylene and polypropylene plastics.
